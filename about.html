About The EMPOWERMENT CIRCLE COMMUNITY

ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Demiladeola Fasasi, fondly known as Demi, is a visionary leader and servant of God whose work is deeply rooted in her faith and obedience to divine calling. As the founder of TECCOM, she has dedicated her life to empowering individuals and communities through selfless service, spiritual growth, and holistic development.
A Vision from God : The inspiration to establish TECCOM was divinely given to Demi by God. She received this vision as a call to build a community centered on love, selflessness, and empowerment. Guided by her unwavering faith, she has committed herself to carrying out this divine mission, believing that the organization is not just her dream but a mandate from God to transform lives and bring hope to a world in need.
Faith and Purpose : Demi’s Christian faith is the cornerstone of her leadership and life’s work. As someone deeply rooted in Christ, she draws strength, guidance, and wisdom from her relationship with God. Her faith not only inspires her actions but also shapes the values and objectives of TECCOM. Demi views the organization as a platform to glorify God by equipping others to fulfill their God-given potential.
A Leader with a Purpose: Demi’s purpose transcends personal ambition. She is dedicated to: Obeying God’s Call: By building TECCOM, she is living out her faith and honoring the vision entrusted to her by God. Creating a Selfless World: Demi strives to encourage people to prioritize helping others without expecting anything in return, reflecting Christ's example of selfless love. Empowering Lives: Through TECCOM, she seeks to impact lives holistically—financially, spiritually, academically, relationally, and personally. .

Vision Behind TELECOM

Demi believes TECCOM is God’s tool for building a world where love and empowerment take precedence. The organization is a testament to her obedience to God’s vision and her commitment to uplifting humanity. The white and green logo represents purity, hope, and growth, symbolic of the divine inspiration behind its creation. Milestones and Achievements Divinely Inspired Founder: TECCOM is not just an organization but a movement born from God’s directive to Demi. Launch Theme: The theme, New Horizons of Empowerment, reflects the limitless possibilities that God’s vision offers for individuals and communities. Collaborative Leadership: Demi’s ability to unite influential speakers, such as Ambassador Eunice Ame and Mr. Benjamin Bright, underscores her dedication to fulfilling God’s purpose. .
